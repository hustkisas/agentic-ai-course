# Weekly review + plan next week

!!! success "Weekly ritual (every Sunday)"
    Scheduled: **2026-07-12** · Week 1 · Phase 0. This is a habit, not a lesson — 30 minutes, every week. It's the single highest-leverage thing for someone who struggles with time management.

## Why this exists

Plans don't fail all at once — they fail through **silent slippage**: one missed session, unacknowledged, then two, then the whole thing feels hopeless. A short weekly review catches slippage while it's cheap to fix and keeps the plan honest. Falling behind is normal; letting it accumulate invisibly is what kills learning plans.

## The 5-question review (do this out loud or in writing)

1. **What did I actually finish this week vs what I planned?** Be specific — which lessons, which exercise, the project?
2. **What slipped, and why?** No guilt — just cause. (Too ambitious? Interrupted? Stuck on something?)
3. **Reschedule the slippage now.** Move unfinished items to specific slots next week. Don't leave them floating.
4. **Did I actually build, or just read?** Reading feels productive but the milestone projects are the real learning. Are you doing them?
5. **Are next week's calendar blocks set?** Open Google Calendar and confirm the study events exist. If not, add them now.
