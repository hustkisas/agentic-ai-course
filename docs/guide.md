# How to use this site

- **Study from anywhere:** this is a static site — open the URL on your laptop or phone.
- **Follow the nav in order**, or use search (top bar) to jump to a topic.
- **Do the milestone projects.** Reading is not learning; building is. The projects are the real curriculum.
- **When a page is just an outline**, ask your coach (Claude) to teach that lesson — it'll be written into the page and pushed live.
- **Falling behind is normal.** Ask your coach to reschedule; the calendar file can be regenerated with new dates.

## Weekly ritual (every Sunday)

1. What did I actually finish vs plan?
2. Reschedule anything that slipped — don't let it pile up silently.
3. Confirm next week's calendar blocks exist.
